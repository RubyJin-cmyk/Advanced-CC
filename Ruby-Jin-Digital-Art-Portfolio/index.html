A Message from Future Ruby Jin (Renayu Jin)

Ten years ago, I arrived in an unfamiliar place to study and experienced my first confession. These two moments still shape me today: the determination to keep learning, and the lessons from failure. Over the years, I have faced challenges both small—like fixing a broken light bulb—and large, like my restless desire to always travel, which often distracted me from my work. Now, I feel at home in the fields I love. I never stop creating, even when it brings pain, because it also brings meaning.
