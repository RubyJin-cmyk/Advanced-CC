Education

I’m currently a sophomore exploring the space where art meets technology. My projects often blend illustration, interactive sketches, and creative coding, allowing me to experiment with new ways of making ideas tangible.

Hobbies & Interests

I enjoy illustration and sketching, and after discovering creative technology, I’ve also become interested in physical making—working with Arduino, soldering, and hands-on experimentation. Combining digital and physical creation has become an important part of my practice.

Career Goals

I hope to work in creative coding, interactive media, or digital art, where I can combine artistic expression with technical skills to create experiences that feel playful, thoughtful, and personal.

Reflections on Technology & Coding

Coding and technology feel like new brushes in my creative toolkit. They allow me to transform ideas—whether digital sketches or interactive installations— into experiences that can be seen, touched, or interacted with, giving form to concepts in ways traditional media cannot.
