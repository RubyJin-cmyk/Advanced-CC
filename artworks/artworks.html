2025/9/16

Hey Pig

In this illustration, I depict a pair of shoes exhibited at the FIT Museum. I want to use this item to metaphor a relationship. The text comes from a song I listened to while painting. It conveys a mix of contempt and provocation toward the other person, yet also an intimate helplessness.

2025/7/26

Noa

This illustration is based on my initial concept for Noa, a bio android “imperfect” due to design flaws. She gradually understands herself and envies humans’ ability to connect physically and spiritually.

Untitled 2

This piece explores the fragile tension between two people, where intimacy and violence coexist. The arrow piercing the heart becomes a symbol of this duality.

Untitled 1

Inspired by Helter Skelter, this work reflects obsession with appearance and fame. The strawberry eye mask and golden face embody allure and decay, while the crown suggests distorted glory.
